Email Configuration (EmailJS)

One-time setup — takes about 5 minutes:
  1. Go to emailjs.com and create a free account.
  2. In EmailJS → Email Services, add your email provider (Gmail, Outlook, etc.) and copy the Service ID.
  3. In EmailJS → Email Templates, click Create New Template. Set the To Email field to doughordering@cornellyqueenanne.com. Use the variables below in the template body, then save and copy the Template ID.
  4. In EmailJS → Account → General, copy your Public Key.
  5. Paste all three values below and click Save.
Available template variables:
{{order_date}}  {{delivery_date}}  {{items_list}}  {{total_cost}}  {{notes}}  {{location}}  {{pasta_shape}} {{quantity}} {{price_per_bag}} (legacy single-item)
⚠ EmailJS not configured

Email Template — Suggested Body

Copy this into your EmailJS template body. Paste as-is; EmailJS fills in the {{variables}} automatically.
Hi Production Kitchen, A new pasta order has been placed from {{location}}. Order Date: {{order_date}} Delivery Date: {{delivery_date}} Items Ordered: {{items_list}} Total Cost: ${{total_cost}} Special Notes: {{notes}} Thank you, {{location}}
