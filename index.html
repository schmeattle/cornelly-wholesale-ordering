Email Configuration (EmailJS)

One-time setup — takes about 5 minutes:
  1. Go to emailjs.com and create a free account.
  2. In EmailJS → Email Services, add your email provider and copy the Service ID.
  3. In EmailJS → Email Templates, create a template with To Email set to doughordering@cornellyqueenanne.com, then copy the Template ID.
  4. In EmailJS → Account → General, copy your Public Key.
  5. Paste all three values below and click Save.
Available variables: {{order_date}} {{delivery_date}} {{items_list}} {{total_cost}} {{notes}} {{location}}
